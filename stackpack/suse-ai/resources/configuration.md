## Installation

Click **Install** to install the SUSE AI Observability StackPack. After installation, follow the instructions here and in the [SUSE AI Observability documentation](https://l.stackstate.com/suse-ai-setup) to complete the setup.

The StackPack requires OpenTelemetry data with GenAI semantic conventions and SUSE AI resource attributes. Ensure your applications are instrumented accordingly and that the OpenTelemetry collector is configured to send data to SUSE Observability.
